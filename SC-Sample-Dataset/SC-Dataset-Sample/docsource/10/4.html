Hi there,

The alfresco.log is defined in a jar file.

open the following jar and you will find log4j.properties, in which you will see "log4j.appender.File.File=alfresco.log"

.

 

jar location = "cmdbuild\WEB-INF\lib\alfresco-web-service-client-3.4.e.jar"

.

 

So, this defines to produce alfresco.log.

hope help.
