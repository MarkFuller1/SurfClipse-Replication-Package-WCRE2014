        javax.naming.NamingException: Failed in WD JNDI lookup

        shifa arshad
        Nov 23, 2011 7:01 AM
        Currently Being Moderated

        Hi,

         

        I have a newly created web dynpro i-view. After transporting the EPA files related to the i-view to production server, We are getting errors

        javax.naming.NamingException: Failed in WD JNDI lookup. javax.naming.NameNotFoundException: No child found in WebDynproContext with name <name of i-view>

        I need to know that  after transporting EPA files ,Is there some procedure to be followed to avoid this error? I read from another post that while creating new i-View, I have to click on the refresh button in the WD iView creation wizard to invalidate JNDI cache. This was not done while creation of i-view. So Is there some other solution as the transport of EPA files has already happened?

            Hi,

             

            This happens mainly when you are having your Production with multiple nodes (with load balancing). There are two ways to cope up with the problem.

            1) Take the J2ee engine restart.(Preferable)

            2) Create the WDJ iviews again in production system. In the iview creation wizard just "refresh" the list of WDJ application.

            Bharat Mistry.
