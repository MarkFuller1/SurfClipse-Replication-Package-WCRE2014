GlassFish and JEE – Common Error – Caused by: javax.naming.NamingException: Lookup failed for …

April 11, 2011 by aquaryus

Hi there!

Just a quick note. Sometimes, when programming in JEE we come across the always popular “Caused by: javax.naming.NamingException: Lookup failed for” error.

While sometimes this error is very hard to resolve, other times it is actually very simple. A common mistake that leads to this error comes from “injections” in your JEE code. In other words, we simply do not indicate the right class to inject due to simply forgetting that in JEE, injections are done through the “local” and “remote” interfaces!!

So check your imports before you get this error again!!
