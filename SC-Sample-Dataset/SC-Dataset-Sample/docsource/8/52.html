Hi Bill,

While you are editing code it is best to turn off the auto-parsing feature in Jigloo by clicking on the round green icon at the top of the Outline for the form. The icon should turn red, and Jigloo will only re-parse your code when you click on the form again. Hope that makes sense.

If you want to email the code to me (jonathan at cloudgarden dot com) I will see if I can fix Jigloo to get rid of some of those errors, or at least generate less log output.
