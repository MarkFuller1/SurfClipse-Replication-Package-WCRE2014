Event ID 1114 - Could not add printer connection to printer

Document ID: CTX107273   /   Created On: Dec 26, 2005   /   Updated On: Oct 31, 2013
Average Rating: (20 ratings)

More Information

A host-based printer or host printer is a printer that relies on the processor of the host computer to generate printable pages. Most host-based printers, in the market today, use the Graphics Device Interface (GDI) built into Windows. Because they do not need a powerful processor of their own, host-based printers tend to be less expensive than conventional printers. Because they share the processor of your computer, they might perform slowly or slow down the work of the host computer. The speed these printers operate depends on how powerful the host computer is and how occupied it is with other operations.
