Thats one of the million JDK printing bugs. Since JDK 1.5, Printing is next to impossible to use thanks to bugs in the JDKs. So far Sun hasn't managed to fix these bugs.

As a result, the next release contains our own printing-system-independent page-setup dialog. We cannot do much about the real printing, but at least we can decouple the page-setup functionality to guarantee that it is usable.
