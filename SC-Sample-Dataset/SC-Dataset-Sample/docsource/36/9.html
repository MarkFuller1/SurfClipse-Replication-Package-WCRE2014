GroovyWrapper and @Grapes

Hi,

After compiling a Groovy-script that contains

@Grapes([
        @Grab(group='org.xerial',module='sqlite-jdbc',version='3.7.2'),
        @GrabConfig(systemClassLoader=true)
])

The resulting jar causes a stacktrace - see below.

When running under Groovy, no problems.

Created a workaround by commenting out @Grapes in my script, adding sqlite-jdbc-3.7.2.jar to $GROOVY_HOME/lib and add a line to GroovyWrapper to include this jar, but I hope there is a better solution....
