    SOCOM produces no sound from PS3. Why?

    This is not a tech support question. I'm not looking for a fix just trying to understand the problem.

    If SOCOM outputs DTS 5.1 on the Optical cable, will the MixAmp decode it and produce sound?

    All games tested from companies other than Sony work fine with A40s and Mixamp. Could the failure of SOCOM be caused by the audio output protocol?

    My AV receiver (Integra DTR-5-5) does produce sound from the speakers and it decodes Dolby and DTS.

    Thanks in advance

    The MixAmp doesn't support Dolby DTS at this time. If you're playing on the PS3 and want to use the MixAmp, you'll have to change the settings to output by Dolby Digital in the audio settings.

    GOT IT! Thanks

    The 3.15 and probably earlier versions of the PS3 OS work differently.

    Under Sound Settings->Audio Output Settings->Optical Digital(in my case)->UNCHECK DTS 5.1 Ch

    Apparently if DTS 5.1 is a selected format than it will supercede Dolby Digital 5.1 Ch.

    Thanks for the hint. It works now even if I have to turn off the DTS.
