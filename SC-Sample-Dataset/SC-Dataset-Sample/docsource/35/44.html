          Bela Ban           added a comment - 08/Apr/10 6:22 AM

          You have a few issues in your config:

          #1 TCP.start_port should be 7800

          #2 TCPPING's port_range is 4, which means that you'll scan ports 7800-7804

          #3 FD_SOCK's start_port is 7803, which is within the above range

          I suggest you pick a different start_port for FD_SOCK.
