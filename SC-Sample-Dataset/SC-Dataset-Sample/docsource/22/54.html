    On 16/10/2011 10:19 a.m., Rick Mugridge wrote:
     
    Yes, it's a TimeStamp.

    It's a pity that the error didn't give you some more information. The problem is with the "!" at the start of your table. That means that all special characters, such as ${} are left as is, rather than substituted.

    So instead use:

    |Execute Procedure | PKG_GTR_BUSINESS.createtradeobject |
    | ACCOUNT | DATE |
    | 1234 |   2011-08-20 14:22:48.384447  |

    Cheers, Rick
