Update to this issue. After further investigation it looks like this was triggered after a ClassCastException was thrown by RemoteWebElement.findElements() in the previous Test.

What steps will reproduce the problem?

Call driver.findElements(By.tagName(String tagName) using RemoteWebDriver, e.g.

List<WebElement> listItems = driver.findElement(By.id("treediv_camtree")).findElements(By.tagName("tr"));

for (WebElement item: listItems) {
   final List<WebElement> spanItems = item.findElements(By.tagName("span"));
   ...
}
