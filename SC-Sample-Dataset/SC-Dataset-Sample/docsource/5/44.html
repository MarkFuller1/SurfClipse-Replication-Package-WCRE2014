the vm options are set in <nbinstaldir>/etc/netbeans.conf
-J-Xms32m is setting a minimum -- do not raise it --
-J-Xmx1024m -- sets that max

raise the max, not the min

the problem could also be your code trying to deal with too big of an
image???
